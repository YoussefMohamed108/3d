How It's Made

The Forging Process

Design

Each figure begins as concept art, refined into a precision 3D sculpt with every detail — cloth folds, armour rivets, facial expression — built for print.

Print

Our MSLA resin printers work at 35-micron resolution — less than half a human hair — building each figure layer by transparent layer in photopolymer resin.

Finish

Support removal, sanding, and multi-stage priming prepare the surface. Hand-painting with miniature brushes and airbrush brings the character to vivid life.
