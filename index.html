How PrintX Works

Three Steps.
Zero Shortcuts.

Design

Every masterpiece begins as a meticulously crafted digital sculpt. We engineer every microscopic detail—from the texture of armor to subtle facial expressions—laying the foundation for ancient perfection.

Print

Cutting-edge MSLA resin technology takes over. Operating at an ultra-fine 35-micron resolution, our machines forge your character layer by layer, bridging the gap between digital imagination and physical reality.

Finish

The final transformation happens by hand. Through meticulous inspection of every detail to ensure Pharaonic-level precision, each figure is then packaged in a suitable box that completes the experience.
